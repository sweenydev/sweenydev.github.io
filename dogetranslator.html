Have you ever wanted to talk like a doge, but the words just won't come out? Embarrassing yourself in front of friends and colleagues?
I've found myself in this situation many more times than I care to admit. That is why I have created "Doge Translator", the answer to all of your woes.
Simply enter your human language phrase into the text box, and click the "Doge it!" button. Then, in a matter of milliseconds, the state of the art doge algorithm will convert your phrase into doge speak! If you aren't satisfied, just click the button again for infinite doge possibilities!
