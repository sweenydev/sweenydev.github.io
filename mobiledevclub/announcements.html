First Meeting: Getting Started

February 14th, 2018

Andrew Johnson

Hey everybody! I'm going to be leading Mobile Dev Club this semester, and our first meeting is tonight! We will be having our first Mobile Development Club meeting tonight at 8:00 PM in Stevenson 401a.

What is this club?
This club will be focused on learning mobile development concepts in Ionic, a super cool and easy to learn application development framework targeted at building hybrid mobile applications (meaning that these apps will compile to Android, iOS, Windows Phone, etc.). If you haven't heard of it, I strongly recommend you check it out!

What are we doing tonight?
In tonight's meeting, I will walk everybody through setting up an Ionic environment, and building your first simple app. You will walk away from this meeting with your own working app, no experience required!

What to Bring
A laptop (Windows or Mac is fine)
A mobile device (Totally optional! It's just cool to see the app on a real device)
A cable to connect that device to your laptop (Again, totally optional!)

So if you have even a passing interest in learning mobile development, be sure to come check us out!
